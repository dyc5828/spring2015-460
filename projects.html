D-Health

D-Health with ITP460 aims to create a dashboard to help both parents and physicians track developmental milestones. The app will help parents visualize data taken from the hospital electronic health record (EHR) system and from their kids' past medical visits in a way that helps them track and understand their kids' developmental milestones.
